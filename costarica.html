Costa Rica

The rainforest in La Fortuna is lush with greens, plants, and wild life. It also has a waterfall about 70 meters high. It took us several hours to hike through and we also took a sky tram to see the forest from high above.
We stayed at two different areas, the first was by the Arenal Volcano in La Fortuna and is one of the ten most active volcanoes in the world. The other was in Manuel Antonio, a small villa overlooking the ocean.
At any given time, a flood of monkeys would come by our villa and hang out by the pool, trees, and deck. They were pretty darn cute.
One of my favorite meals in Costa Rica is pinto y gallo. This street had one of our favorite restaurants, Cafe Milagro.
