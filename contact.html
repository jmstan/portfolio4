I am inspired to create clean and simple websites. I use semantic code with the latest web standards to create beautiful and functional designs.

I became involved in web development after a long history of working in photography. While I decided not to pursue photography as a career, elements such as layout, design, color, and tech skills kept me wanting to learn more. I got my first taste of being a web developer after taking a course in college and making a site for a friend. I loved it, decided to delve deeper, take courses and teach myself along with looking at what other developers are doing. I spend many hours trying to write the best semantic and efficient code, making flat-file content management, and learning the ins and outs of making a seamless and functional website.
